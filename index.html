BIOGRAPHY

Ge Zhang is a 2nd-year Ph.D. student at School of Computing, Macquarie University. She is interested in developing ML algorithms over graph-structured data to solve real-world problems, especially fraud detection and anomaly detection. Previously, she received Master's in College of Intelligence and Computing at Tianjin University.

Ge Zhang has been selected as one of 2022 Microsoft Research PhD Fellows. She is the only recipient in Australia & New Zealand in 2022.
